Design &
Animation

The core of Katha Rakyat lies in its fluidity. By utilizing creative coding (p5.js), the interface is not static; it breathes as the user interacts with the story.


Each animation is calculated in real-time, ensuring that no two storytelling sessions are visually identical. This mirrors the nature of oral traditions, where every retelling has its own unique flair. The design language uses a dark-mode minimalist aesthetic to make the cultural assets pop with high contrast.
