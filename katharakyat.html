Katha Rakyat x Nyawang Rasa

Dawala

Dawala is a composed folklore character shaped by the serok (strainer), symbolizing the filtering of emotions, and the gamelan, representing soft harmony. His even-tempered nature transforms tension into clarity.

Cepot

Cepot is a lively folklore character shaped by the saringan (juicer), symbolizing the extraction of essence, and the angklung, representing playful rhythm. His energetic nature turns everyday moments into engaging expressions.
